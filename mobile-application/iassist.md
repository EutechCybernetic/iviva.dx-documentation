**iAssist** is the voice assistant in the iviva.DX mobile app. It lets you interact with the app using voice commands.

### Launching iAssist

1. On the **Home Screen** or **Search Screen** , tap the **microphone icon** at the bottom-right to open the iAssist popup.&#x20;
2. Speak naturally to perform actions like navigating, searching, or opening apps.

### Enabling or Configuring iAssist

1. Tap the **hamburger menu** on the Home Screen
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Scroll to the **iAssist Settings** section
