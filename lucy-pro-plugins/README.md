# Lucy Pro Plugins

Lucy is an integration platform designed for your Smart City, Smart Building or Smart Workplace.

She helps you digitise your environment, model it, and drive meaningful outcomes for your business or workplace.

**What You Can Easily Do with Lucy?**

* **Define and model** all of your organisational assets, devices, people, and places
* **Talk to IoT devices** and control system
* **Run orchestrations and workflows** that process events and data in real-time across your organisation and act on them
* **Integrate with third-party services**—whether they run in the cloud or on-premise—and incorporate them into your workflow.
* **Publish RESTfull APIs** for your orchestrations and workflows
* **Push data in real-time** to dashboards, devices, and other interfaces
