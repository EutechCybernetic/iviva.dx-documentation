# Initial Form

The Initial Form section is where you define the starting point for data entry in your workflow. It captures the essential information required to initiate a process, making it a key part of how your application collects and organises task-related details.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/1 (9) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Component 1 - Initial Form Details**

You can manually create a new form by entering a name and a description. This gives you the freedom to design a form layout from scratch, tailored to your unique data capture requirements.

**Component 2 - Sample Initial Form Template**

Choose from a set of sample initial form templates. These templates include predefined fields and structures commonly used in work requests or task submissions.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (28).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

When you click on a template, the form configuration view opens, letting you to review and adjust the form contents to fit your specific needs.

This setup ensures that, whether you're starting from scratch or using a template, you can quickly create a functional, user-friendly form that collects the right information to start your workflow effectively.
