# Others

The **Others** tab offers you advanced input options like NFC and QR scanning.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/1 (87).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Component 1 → Enable NFC**\
When the **Enable NFC** toggle is turned on, the field can grab info from NFC tags. Users just tap an NFC-enabled device or tag, and the field fills in automatically—great for quick, contactless input in places like asset tracking or access control.

**Component 2 → Enable QR**\
Turn on the **Enable QR** toggle to let users scan QR codes right from the form. The QR scanner opens inline, and any information in the code is automatically used to fill the field—quick and easy.
