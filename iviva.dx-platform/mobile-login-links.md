# Mobile Login Links

The **iviva.DX** Mobile App is a powerful, no-code digital transformation platform designed to simplify and accelerate the digitisation of business processes. It enables users to model, execute, and optimise workflows without the need for coding expertise, making it accessible to anyone aiming to improve operational efficiency.

With the **iviva.DX** Mobile App, organisations can streamline their workflows, automate tasks, and improve productivityâ€”all from the convenience of their mobile devices. The app brings the full functionality of the **iviva.DX** platform to your fingertips, enabling seamless business process management anytime, anywhere.

### **Key Features**:

* **No-Code Workflow Automation**: Easily model and automate business processes without any coding knowledge.
* **Mobile Access**: Manage and optimise workflows from anywhere at anytime.
* **Seamless Integration**: Integrates smoothly with existing systems to support a unified digital experience.

The **iviva.DX Mobile App** is available for download on both the **Google Play Store** and **Apple App Store**, ensuring accessibility for both Android and iOS users.

**Component 1:** Displays the **Domain Name** that the mobile app will connect to. This field is pre-filled with the server address where the **iviva.DX** platform is hosted.

**Component 2:** Shows the **User ID** field, which typically represents the login username of the individual attempting to connect the mobile app.

**Component 3:** The **Passcode** field provides a secure entry for the user's mobile access credentials, used during QR-based mobile authentication.

**Component 4:** The **Regenerate QR** button allows users to generate a new QR code for secure and updated mobile device pairing, in case the existing code has expired or requires refreshing.

**Component 5:** Displays the **QR Code** used to scan and connect the mobile app to the **iviva.DX** platform. Scanning this code with the mobile device enables quick and secure login without the need for manual credential entry.
