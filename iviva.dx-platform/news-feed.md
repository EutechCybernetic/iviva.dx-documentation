# News Feed

The **News Feed** feature allows users to publish and share posts directly to their system. It provides a dynamic and engaging way to share news, updates, or other relevant content with a designated audience. This feature enables users to contribute content that is visible within their feed, enhancing communication and interaction within the platform.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/1 (72) (2).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Component 1:** The **Search Bar** allows users to search through existing posts in the **News Feed** using keywords, making it easier to find specific content.

**Component 2:** The **Create Button** enables users to add a new post to the **News Feed**. Clicking this opens a form where users can define post's content, type, group, and publication settings.

**Component 3:** Displays the **Title of the Post** (e.g., "Post 1"), providing a quick reference to the content. _(Part of the post card)_

**Component 4:** Shows the **Post Details**, including post status (e.g., Enabled), post type (e.g., Text Post), group name, and a short content preview, helping users understand the post at a glance. _(Part of the post card)_

**Component 5:** The **Edit Icon** allows users to modify an existing postâ€™s content or metadata. _(Part of the post card)_

**Component 6:** The **Delete Icon** enables users to remove a post from the news feed. _(Part of the post card)_

**Component 7:** **Post Group** allows users to create and manage categorised groups for posts, aiding in content organisation and targeted communication.

**Component 8:** **Post Publication** enables users to assign post groups to specific user groups, ensuring that posts are displayed only to the intended audience.
