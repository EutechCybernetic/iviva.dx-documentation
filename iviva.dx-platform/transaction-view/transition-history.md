# Transition History

The **Transitions History** section provides a detailed log of all your past transitions, allowing you to track and review previous activities  and changes. It offers a clear overview of what actions were taken by whom, and when.

An integrated **search option** allows users to quickly locate specific transactions or tasks<br>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/1 (58).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Transition History</p></figcaption></figure>

* **Component 1 :** \
  **Transition History tab** in the sidebar provides quick navigation to the Transition History section within the page, allowing users to access historical data instantly without scrolling or leaving the current interface.<br>
* **Component 2 :** \
  Displays a **comprehensive list of all recorded transitions**, along with the exact timestamps for when each transition occurred. Additional details such as the user who made the change and the previous and new stages are also shown to provide full context.<br>
* **Component 3 :** \
  Includes a search function for the transition history, enabling users to quickly locate specific transitions using keywords such as stage names or other relevant criteria for easier tracking and reference.
