# Activity History

The **Activity History** section provides a detailed record of all your interactions and actions within the system, including updates, changes, and important events. This helps you stay informed about past activities and track progress over time.

You can easily search through the history using the integrated search option to quickly locate specific activities.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/1 (59) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Activity History</p></figcaption></figure>

* **Component 1 :** \
  The **Activity History tab** in the sidebar provides quick navigation to the activity history section within the page, allowing users to access a detailed log of actions without needing to scroll or search manually.<br>
* **Component 2 :** \
  Includes a search function for the activity history, enabling users to easily find specific actions.<br>
* **Component 3 :** \
  Displays a comprehensive list of all activities performed by users, along with timestamps for when each action occurred. Additional details such as the type of action (e.g., created, submitted) and the user involved are also included for improved traceability.
