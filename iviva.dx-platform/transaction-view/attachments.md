# Attachments

The Attachment section provides a centralized view of all files and documents associated with a transaction. Any attachments uploaded through the **File Uploader** or **Image Picker** appear here, offering easy access to important images, PDFs, and other supported file types. To manage or update these attachments, use the corresponding **Attachment** or **Image Picker** field based on your needs.<br>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/1 (31).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



* **Component 1 :** When **Attachments** is clicked in the sidebar, the page scrolls directly to the Attachments section, allowing quick and convenient access to all related files.
* **Component 2 :** This section displays all files attached to the transaction, including documents and images added via the **File Uploader** or **Image Picker,** providing a clear overview of all related attachments in one place.
